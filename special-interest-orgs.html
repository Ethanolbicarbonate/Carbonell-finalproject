Our Student Organizations:
Fostering Community and Growth

Be a Part of Our Community

At the College of Information and Communications Technology (CICT), we embrace a holistic approach to education. Our belief extends beyond the confines of lecture halls and textbooks. It’s about fostering a dynamic ecosystem where students not only gain knowledge but also forge meaningful connections and develop practical skills. Our student organizations serve as vibrant hubs—each one a microcosm of passion, creativity, and growth. Whether you’re immersed in lines of code, sketching user interfaces, or troubleshooting complex tech challenges, there’s a welcoming space for you.

Special Interest

CYB:ORG - Cyb Robotics Organization

At CYB:ORG, we believe that all great endeavors begin with a dream—a purpose that fuels our actions. Our mission? To turn those dreams into reality. As a community of dreamers, tinkerers, and innovators, we dare to explore the uncharted territories of robotics.
Imagine a place where curiosity knows no bounds, where wonder takes flight. That's CYB:ORG. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just starting out, we invite you to join our journey. Here, you'll learn, create, and innovate alongside fellow students who share your passion for robotics.

Link.exe

Our Purpose and Passion
Enrich: At Link.exe, we believe in enriching minds across disciplines. Dive into the world of ideas, critical thinking, and problem-solving. Let’s enrich your understanding and broaden your horizons.

Uphold: We uphold the values of integrity, empathy, and resilience. As a tight-knit community, we support each other’s endeavors. From organizing collaborative events to fostering cross-disciplinary dialogues, we’re committed to making a positive impact.

Generate: Innovation is our heartbeat. Link.exe generates ideas, sparks conversations, and incubates solutions. Let’s explore uncharted territories, challenge assumptions, and create something new together.

Serve: Beyond self-improvement, we serve our campus and community. Link.exe believes that our collective efforts can create meaningful change. Whether it’s through volunteering, organizing events, or simply lending a helping hand, we’re dedicated to leaving a positive mark.
