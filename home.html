Undergraduate Programs Learn More

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

Dive into algorithms, software development, and artificial intelligence.

Build real-world applications and collaborate on exciting projects.

Prepare for a career in software engineering, data analysis, or research.

BSCS Overview:
The BSCS program equips students with a strong foundation in computer science theory and practical skills. You’ll delve into algorithms, data structures, software development, and artificial intelligence. Whether you aspire to create cutting-edge applications, work in research, or analyze complex data, BSCS prepares you for a dynamic career in technology.

Key Areas:
Software Development: Learn programming languages (JavaScript, Python, C++) and build robust software applications.
Data Science: Explore data analytics, machine learning, and big data processing.
Theoretical Concepts: Understand algorithm design, complexity analysis, and computational theory.

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

Master web development, database management, and cybersecurity.

Learn to solve complex IT challenges and enhance user experiences.

Launch your career as a systems analyst, network administrator, or IT consultant.

BSIT Overview:
BSIT combines technical expertise with business acumen. You’ll master web development, database management, and cybersecurity. As an IT professional, you’ll bridge the gap between technology and organizational goals. Whether you’re interested in systems administration, network security, or user experience design, BSIT opens doors to diverse career paths.

Key Areas:
Web Development: Create responsive websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks.
Database Management: Design and optimize databases for efficient data storage and retrieval.
Cybersecurity: Learn to protect systems from threats, secure networks, and implement best practices.

Bachelor of Science in Information Systems

Explore the intersection of technology and business processes.

Gain skills in systems analysis, project management, and database design.

Become a valuable asset in organizations that rely on efficient information systems.

BLIS Overview:
BSIS focuses on the strategic use of technology within organizations. You’ll explore systems analysis, project management, and information architecture. BSIS graduates play a crucial role in designing, implementing, and maintaining efficient information systems that drive business success.

Key Areas:
Systems Analysis: Understand user requirements, model business processes, and design effective solutions.
Project Management: Learn to lead IT projects, manage resources, and meet deadlines.
Database Design: Create relational databases, ensure data integrity, and optimize performance.

Bachelor of Science in Entertainment and Multimedia Computing

Combine creativity and technology in the world of multimedia.

Develop skills in animation, game design, interactive media, and virtual reality.

Create captivating content for entertainment, education, and marketing.

BSEMC Overview:
BSEMC merges creativity with technology, empowering you to craft captivating digital experiences. Dive into animation, game design, virtual reality, and interactive media. As a BSEMC graduate, you'll contribute to the ever-evolving world of multimedia.

Key Areas:
Animation: Create both 2D and 3D animations for films, games, and advertisements.
Game Design: Explore game mechanics, level design, and compelling storytelling.
Interactive Media: Develop user-friendly interfaces, mobile apps, and immersive digital content.

Bachelor of Library and Information Science

Delve into library science, cataloging, and information organization.

Learn about digital libraries, archival management, and information retrieval.

Contribute to preserving knowledge and facilitating access to information.

BLIS Overview:
BLIS prepares students for careers in libraries, archives, and information centers. Beyond traditional library science, BLIS covers digital libraries, metadata, and information organization. If you’re passionate about preserving knowledge, facilitating access to information, and promoting literacy, BLIS offers a rewarding path.

Key Areas:
Cataloging and Classification: Learn how to organize and describe information resources.
Digital Libraries: Explore digital preservation, metadata standards, and online access.
Archival Management: Understand archival principles, records management, and historical preservation.

Graduate Programs Learn More

Master in Information Technology

Create, evaluate, and administer globally competitive computer-based systems.

Apply advanced technical knowledge to evaluate existing technology and develop new software applications.

Design marketable IT products based on research outputs.

Manage information technology systems tailored to organizational needs.

Recognize opportunities for career growth and lifelong learning as IT professionals

MIT Overview:
The Master in Information Technology (MIT) program offers an advanced two-year graduate curriculum that delves into both theoretical and practical aspects of information and communications technology. As an MIT student, you’ll explore cutting-edge concepts and technologies, preparing you for roles in systems integration, systems administration, systems planning, and systems implementation.
This program emphasizes depth and guided research, with a focus on publishing research and development outputs. Areas of specialization align with current IT trends. The MIT program aims to equip students with knowledge in IT concepts, techniques, and principles relevant to organizations and society.

Key Areas:
Advanced Database Organization: Explore the intricacies of database management and optimization.
Computer Information Networks: Understand network architecture, protocols, and security.
Advanced Operating Systems: Dive into the complexities of operating system design and management.
Systems Design and Implementation: Develop expertise in creating robust and efficient IT systems.
Information Security Administration: Focus on safeguarding data and systems from threats.
Data Warehouse Technology and Implementation Strategies: Explore data warehousing and analytics.

Student Success Stories

Karlo Roel

From Student to Millionaire CEO

Karlo Roel, once a determined BSCS student at CICT, has now ascended to the ranks of millionaires. His journey began with late-night coding sessions in the campus lab, fueled by passion and a vision.

After graduation, he founded a tech startup that revolutionized e-commerce logistics. Today, as the CEO of “PaBaby,” a global logistics giant, Karlo Roel continues to support his alma mater. His generous donations have funded scholarships, research projects, and state-of-the-art labs within the college.

His advice to aspiring entrepreneurs? “Dream big, work harder, and never forget where you came from.”

Steven Ken

Gaming Guru Turned Billionaire

Steven Ken, a BSCS graduate, discovered his niche early on: gaming. His YouTube channel, “redfurygaming7077,” exploded in popularity as he fearlessly dissected game mechanics, roasted developers and innocent players, and engaged in epic trash talk with his Alpha Wolf Behavior.

His charisma and unfiltered commentary resonated with millions of viewers. Steven’s channel became a gold mine, attracting sponsorships, merchandise deals, and a dedicated fan base. Today, he’s a billionaire, investing in game studios and launching his own gaming platform.

His secret? “Authenticity and a thick skin. Haters gonna hate, but passion pays the bills.”

Janfloyd

Grooming Guru and Community Hero

Janfloyd, a BSCS graduate, took an unconventional path. Instead of coding, he found his calling in grooming services. Armed with knowledge about information organization and customer behavior, he founded “JapJaps Grooming Services.”

His pet spa became a sensation, not just for its luxurious treatments but also for its community impact. Janfloyd hosts free grooming workshops for underprivileged kids, teaching them empathy and responsibility through caring for animals.

His motto? “Grooming hearts, one paw at a time.” Children adore him, and his business thrives on compassion.
